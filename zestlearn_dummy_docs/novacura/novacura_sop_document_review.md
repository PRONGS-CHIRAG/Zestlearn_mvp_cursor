# NovaCura Biotech GmbH — SOP for Internal Document Review

## Purpose
This SOP defines the process for internal review of experiment summaries, protocol updates, and supporting scientific documentation before handover to Regulatory Affairs or Quality Assurance.

## Scope
Applies to R&D, Bioinformatics, Regulatory Affairs, and QA.

## Current Process
1. Scientist completes experiment summary in shared drive template.
2. Supporting files are attached manually.
3. Team lead reviews summary for completeness.
4. Bioinformatics adds analysis note where needed.
5. Regulatory Affairs receives final packet by email.
6. QA performs final spot check before archival.

## Current Pain Points
- Reviewers spend significant time checking formatting and completeness manually.
- Version names are inconsistent across teams.
- Prior experiment summaries are hard to retrieve from shared folders.
- Similar comments are repeated across multiple reviews.
- Regulatory handover is often delayed because context is incomplete.

## Tools in Use
- Shared network folders
- Excel trackers
- Email threads
- Word templates

## Risks
- Missing supporting context in handover
- Delays in approval timelines
- Duplicate effort across teams

## Improvement Opportunity
An AI assistant could summarize submitted packets, flag missing sections, and surface similar past documents for reviewers.
