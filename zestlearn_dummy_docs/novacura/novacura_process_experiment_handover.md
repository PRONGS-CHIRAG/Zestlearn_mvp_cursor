# NovaCura Biotech GmbH — Process Description: Experiment Handover to Regulatory

## Objective
Describe the current workflow for handing experiment outputs from R&D to Regulatory Affairs.

## Workflow
1. Scientist prepares experiment summary document.
2. Analysis outputs are exported and attached manually.
3. Team lead reviews scientific interpretation.
4. Additional clarifications are requested over email.
5. Final file bundle is uploaded to shared folder.
6. Regulatory Affairs receives email notification and begins review.

## Issues Observed
- There is no consistent summary layer across submissions.
- Clarification loops happen over email and are hard to track.
- Reviewers struggle to quickly identify what changed from previous versions.
- Important context is often buried in attachment names or email chains.

## Desired Future State
- Faster review preparation
- Easier retrieval of past context
- Better knowledge continuity across teams
