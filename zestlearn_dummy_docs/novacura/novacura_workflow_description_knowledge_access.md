# NovaCura Biotech GmbH — Workflow Description: Accessing Prior Experiment Knowledge

## Current State
When a team member needs context from a past experiment, they typically:
1. Search shared folders manually
2. Ask colleagues for document links
3. Review old meeting notes and email threads
4. Reconstruct the context from multiple files

## Typical Frictions
- Search depends heavily on memory of file names
- Important details are buried in long documents
- Prior reasoning is not captured consistently
- New team members struggle the most

## Business Problem
Knowledge retrieval is too slow and too dependent on individual employees.

## Potential AI Opportunity
A knowledge assistant that summarizes prior studies and retrieves the most relevant supporting documents could reduce friction and improve continuity.
