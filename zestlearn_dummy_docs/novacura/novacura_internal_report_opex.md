# NovaCura Biotech GmbH — Internal Operations Report

**Period:** January 2026

## Summary
Documentation-related review work has increased significantly with the growth of parallel R&D activities. Teams report rising coordination overhead and longer time spent locating prior context.

## Findings
- Average time to compile a handover packet increased by 18%.
- Regulatory review kickoff was delayed in 6 of 14 recent cases due to missing or fragmented context.
- Scientists reported repeated requests for clarifications on previously discussed background.
- Bioinformatics staff spend time rewriting analysis summaries for non-technical reviewers.

## Operational Impact
- Delayed cross-team handovers
- Higher dependency on senior staff for context recall
- Reduced efficiency in document-heavy workflows

## Recommended Areas to Explore
- AI summarization for handover packets
- Internal semantic search across experiment summaries
- Template-driven knowledge capture
