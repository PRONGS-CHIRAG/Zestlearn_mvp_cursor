# HelixPharm Solutions AG — Process Description: Review and Routing of Internal Documents

## Objective
Describe how internal quality and operational documents are currently reviewed and assigned.

## Workflow
1. New document arrives in inbox or shared folder.
2. Coordinator opens file and determines category.
3. Metadata is checked manually.
4. Appropriate reviewer is selected.
5. Reviewer performs completeness and policy check.
6. Status is entered into Excel tracker.
7. Final approval is sent by email.

## Pain Points
- Reviewers spend time on low-value repetitive intake tasks.
- The routing step depends on manual judgment.
- Priority items are not always surfaced early.
- Documents with missing sections still move too far into the process.

## Desired Future State
- Faster first-pass triage
- Better prioritization
- Less repetitive effort for documentation teams
