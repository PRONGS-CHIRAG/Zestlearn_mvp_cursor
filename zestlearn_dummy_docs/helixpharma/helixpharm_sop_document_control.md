# HelixPharm Solutions AG — SOP for Document Control and Review

## Purpose
Define the standard process for intake, review, classification, and approval of internal operational and quality documents.

## Scope
Applies to Operations, Manufacturing Support, Quality Control, Regulatory Documentation, and Compliance.

## Current Workflow
1. Documents are received through email or shared folders.
2. Coordinator checks whether the correct template was used.
3. Document is manually classified.
4. Reviewer is assigned based on topic.
5. Reviewer checks completeness and compliance notes.
6. Approval is tracked in an Excel log.

## Current Pain Points
- High volume of repetitive review work
- Manual classification slows routing
- Approval queues are difficult to prioritize
- Missing metadata leads to back-and-forth emails
- Similar corrections recur across many documents

## Risks
- Delayed internal approvals
- Human error in routing and classification
- Review fatigue in operations teams

## Improvement Opportunity
A document triage copilot could summarize, classify, and route documents faster while keeping a human in the loop.
