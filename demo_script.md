# ZestLearn Investor Demo Script (2–3 min, Loom)

A full script you can follow for a Loom recording aimed at investors.

---

## INTRO — The Hook (0:00 - 0:20)

> "Pharma and biotech companies spend over $2 billion a year on management consulting — yet 70% of those engagements end with a PDF that sits in a drawer.
>
> ZestLearn changes that. We're building an AI-powered consulting copilot purpose-built for pharma and biotech SMEs — the companies that need strategic guidance the most but can't afford a Big Four retainer.
>
> Let me show you how it works."

**[On screen: Land on the ZestLearn landing page.]**

---

## SECTION 1 — Assessment (0:20 - 0:45)

> "Everything starts with a 60-second assessment. The company tells us who they are — their size, sector focus, current pain points, and what they're hoping AI can help with."

**[On screen: Fill out the assessment form with realistic pharma data — e.g., company name "NovaCura Therapeutics", 85 employees, drug development, bottleneck in document review.]**

> "This isn't a generic intake form. Every answer feeds directly into a personalized workspace and shapes every AI recommendation downstream. No two companies get the same output."

**[On screen: Submit the form. Show the redirect to the workspace.]**

---

## SECTION 2 — Workspace Overview (0:45 - 1:05)

> "Here's the workspace. Think of it as the company's private AI strategy room."

**[On screen: Pan across the Overview panel — company name, sector, team size, focus areas displayed cleanly.]**

> "The overview pulls directly from the assessment — no placeholder data, no templates. This is NovaCura's workspace, built for NovaCura's problems."

---

## SECTION 3 — Document Upload & AI Processing (1:05 - 1:30)

> "Now, the team uploads their internal documents — SOPs, meeting notes, process descriptions. ZestLearn extracts the text, generates an AI summary, and auto-tags each document by category — compliance, workflow, bottleneck, operations."

**[On screen: Switch to the Documents tab. Upload one document (e.g., a process review PDF). Wait briefly for the AI summary and tags to appear.]**

> "This is important — the AI doesn't just store files. It *understands* them. Every document becomes context that makes the consultant smarter."

---

## SECTION 4 — AI Consultant Chat (1:30 - 2:00)

> "Now the real magic — the AI Consultant. This is a context-aware chat grounded in the company's own data. It pulls from the assessment, every uploaded document, and the full conversation history."

**[On screen: Switch to the Chat tab. Type a question like "What are the top AI opportunities for our team?" or click a prompt starter. Wait for the response.]**

> "Notice — this isn't generic ChatGPT output. The recommendations reference NovaCura's actual bottlenecks, their team size, their regulatory context. And the user can choose which AI model powers the response."

**[On screen: Briefly show the model selector. Then click the "Listen" button on the assistant reply to demo voice playback.]**

> "We've also added voice — the consultant can read its recommendations aloud, which is great for hands-free review or accessibility."

---

## SECTION 5 — Report Generation & PDF (2:00 - 2:20)

> "When the company is ready, they generate a full strategic report — pulling together everything: documents, chat insights, and workspace context — into a structured, professional deliverable."

**[On screen: Switch to the Reports tab. Click "Generate Report". Wait for it to render. Show the markdown report briefly — headings, sections, recommendations.]**

> "And with one click, they download it as a polished PDF — ready to share with leadership or a board."

**[On screen: Click "Download Report as PDF". Show the download happening.]**

---

## SECTION 6 — Collective Learning (2:20 - 2:35)

> "Here's what makes ZestLearn a platform, not just a chatbot. Our Collective Learning layer captures anonymized insights, trends, and patterns across every engagement — while preserving full privacy. No PII is ever exposed."

**[On screen: Switch to the Insights tab. Show a few insight cards with categories and relevance scores.]**

> "Over time, ZestLearn gets smarter for *every* company that uses it. That's a compounding data moat no traditional consultancy can replicate."

---

## CLOSE — The Ask (2:35 - 2:55)

> "So to recap — ZestLearn delivers:
>
> **One**: Personalized AI consulting for pharma SMEs at a fraction of Big Four cost.
>
> **Two**: Context-grounded recommendations, not generic AI output.
>
> **Three**: A collective intelligence layer that compounds with every engagement.
>
> We're live, it works end-to-end, and we built the full MVP in under two weeks.
>
> If you're interested in the future of AI-native consulting for life sciences, I'd love to talk. Thanks for watching."

**[On screen: Stay on the workspace. Smile. End recording.]**

---

## Tips for Recording

- **Pace**: Aim for calm and confident, not rushed. Pause 1–2 seconds between sections.
- **Mouse movement**: Move the cursor deliberately. Don't hover randomly while talking.
- **Pre-load data**: Before recording, run through the full flow once so the workspace already has a few documents, chat messages, and a generated report ready to show. You can still demo the upload and chat live, but having existing data makes the workspace feel alive.
- **Voice playback**: Test the ElevenLabs playback once before recording so you know it works on the first try.
- **Camera**: Keep your face visible in the Loom bubble — investors connect with founders, not just screens.
- **Length**: If you run over 3 minutes, cut the Collective Learning section shorter — the strongest moments are the live chat response and the PDF download.
