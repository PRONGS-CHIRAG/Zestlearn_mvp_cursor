# Fake Pharma / Biotech Companies for ZestLearn Testing

## 1. NovaCura Biotech GmbH

### Type

Mid-sized biotech company

### Location

Munich, Germany

### Focus

Cell and gene therapy support tools, biomarker research, and preclinical data operations

### Company Size

120 employees

### Main Teams

* R&D
* Bioinformatics
* Regulatory Affairs
* Quality Assurance
* Operations

### Business Situation

NovaCura is growing quickly, but its internal knowledge is fragmented across scientists, shared folders, emails, and meeting notes. Teams struggle to retrieve previous experiment context and supporting documentation efficiently.

### Common Problems

* Research knowledge scattered across documents
* Repeated manual summaries of experiment findings
* Slow internal handovers between R&D and regulatory teams
* Difficulty tracking document versions
* Heavy dependence on individual employees for context

### Good ZestLearn Testing Themes

* knowledge fragmentation
* research memory assistant
* document summarization
* semantic search
* AI-assisted internal knowledge base

## 2. HelixPharm Solutions AG

### Type

Pharma SME

### Location

Basel, Switzerland

### Focus

Specialty pharmaceuticals and internal documentation-heavy operational processes

### Company Size

210 employees

### Main Teams

* Operations
* Manufacturing Support
* Quality Control
* Regulatory Documentation
* Compliance

### Business Situation

HelixPharm handles a large volume of internal reports, SOPs, deviation records, and approval documents. A lot of work is still manual, especially document review, categorization, and routing.

### Common Problems

* Manual review of internal documents takes too long
* Teams spend too much time classifying and checking reports
* Approval workflows are slow
* Repetitive documentation work creates bottlenecks
* Employees worry about errors in document handling

### Good ZestLearn Testing Themes

* document triage assistant
* document classification
* summarization copilot
* workflow automation support
* low-risk first AI pilot for operations teams

## 3. Rheonix Life Sciences GmbH

### Type

Life sciences SME

### Location

Berlin, Germany

### Focus

Diagnostic product development and clinical documentation workflows

### Company Size

85 employees

### Main Teams

* Clinical Operations
* Product Development
* Regulatory Affairs
* Project Management
* Data Management

### Business Situation

Rheonix has cross-functional teams working on clinical and product documentation, but coordination is inefficient. Information is stored across spreadsheets, PDFs, email chains, and shared drives, making it hard to maintain a clear overview.

### Common Problems

* Poor visibility across workflows
* Too many status updates handled manually
* Meeting notes and action items get lost
* Project teams duplicate work because information is hard to find
* Documentation and reporting take too much time

### Good ZestLearn Testing Themes

* workflow assistant
* project/document tracking support
* meeting notes summarization
* internal Q&A assistant
* coordination and process optimization
